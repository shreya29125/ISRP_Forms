NON-DEGREE FORM

Instructions for filling up the application form


  1. All fields are mandatory. In case any field is not applicable then please put ‘NA’.
  2. Personal details must be filled as per your passport.
  3. The application will require the following scanned documents in PDF format (size less than 500 kB). Keep the scanned documents ready before you proceed. Documents once uploaded cannot be changed.
    • Passport
    • Academic Transcripts
    • Curriculum Vitae
    • Statement of Research Interest (mandatory for Doctoral Applications)
    • Scholarship details (if applicable)
    • GATE Scorecard (if applicable)
    • Work Experience Details (if applicable)
  4. You will also have to upload your photo and signature in jpg format (size less than 250 kB).
  5. You can click on the 'Save' button in order to save your application and continue at a later stage.
  6. The application will be considered as complete on clicking the 'Apply' button. You will not be able to make any changes after submitting.
  7. The application fee is INR 500. After submitting the application, you will be able to make the payment through a payment gateway. After successful payment, you will be provided with an option to download the payment details in PDF format. Download it and keep it with you for your reference.
  8. Please note that the application fee is exempted for those applicants applying through ICCR/EDCIL(SII).
